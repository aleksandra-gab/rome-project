Why Rome?

Rome, the Eternal City

Rome is called the Eternal City because ancient Romans believed that no matter what happened to the world, or how many empires rose or fell, that Rome would go on forever. The enduring quality of the city continues to this day and it's no surprise with all its beauty and intrigue that Rome remains one of the most beloved tourist destinations in the world.

The best restaurants in Rome

That's Amore

In the heart of Rome, a few steps from the Trevi Fountain, That's Amore is a cozy space inspired by the best traditions of the Roman "trattorias." Day and night, seven days a week, the warm and professional staff serves up the most authentic regional cuisine.

Italian, Mediterranean, European, Romana, Lazio, Central-Italian

La Tavernaccia

La Tavernaccia Da Bruno is an over 50 year old family-run historic Roman restaurant. Over the years it has become a point of reference for all those who love regional cuisine that is simple and natural and helps those rediscover the flavours of the past.

Cousines:

Italian, Pizza, Mediterranean, Romana, Lazio, Central-Italian
